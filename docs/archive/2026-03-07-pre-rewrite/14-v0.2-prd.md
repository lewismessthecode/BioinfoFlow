# v0.2 PRD: Personal WGS Revolution

**Version:** 0.2  
**Status:** Planning  
**Core Theme:** GPU-Accelerated Personal Whole Genome Sequencing

---

## Vision

> **"Run your own WGS at home."**

Enable anyone with a gaming PC (RTX 4080+ & 64GB RAM) to run professional-grade Whole Genome Sequencing analysis at home, in ~1-2 hours, with complete data privacy.

---

## Why This is Explosive 🔥

| Factor | Traditional WGS | Bioinfoflow + Parabricks |
|--------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| **Time** | 20-30+ hours (CPU) | **< 2 hours** (GPU) |
| **Hardware** | HPC/Cloud clusters | Gaming PC (RTX 40xx) |
| **Cost** | $100-500/analysis | Free (Parabricks personal use) |
| **Privacy** | Data leaves your machine | 100% local, never uploaded |
| **Expertise** | DevOps + Bioinformatics | Natural language AI agent |

---

## Target Users

1. **Biohackers / Quantified Self Enthusiasts**
   - Already have WGS data from 23andMe/Nebula/etc
   - Want deeper analysis without uploading data

2. **Genetics Students & Researchers**
   - Learning bioinformatics
   - Need affordable local compute

3. **Privacy-Conscious Individuals**
   - Have raw FASTQ files
   - Refuse cloud-based analysis

4. **Open-Source Community**
   - Want transparent, auditable tools
   - Contribute to decentralized genomics
